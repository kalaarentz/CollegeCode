History of Booktracker

Booktracker is a company full of discoveries. Founded in 1988, Booktracker allows anyone to keep track of any book online. The company has one true motto: "To Keep on Reading."

Other values this company holds is that we put our customers at the center of what we do. We value, challenge and reward our people. We drive excellen sustainable finacial performance. We build our future on our family business foundation.

Leaders of Booktracker

Denny Sasquatch
The founder of Booktracker, he created this company in his parents garage. Meeting Moon Moon in college allowed his idea to become a founder of a company real. His Master's in Computer Science with an emphasis in Web Design and Development helped him create this company in 1988.
Moon Moon
The current CEO, she grew up in the Northwest Territories of Canada. She traveled as a young adult, and met Denny Sasquatch in her final year of graduate school. While Denny Sasquatch was behind the technology, Moon Moon supported the reading aspect of this company.
