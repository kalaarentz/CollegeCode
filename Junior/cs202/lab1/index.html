Grandma's Apple Pie Recipe

Description

One of the best things I remember about my randmother is her delicious apple pies. It will always be my favority and I'll always remmeber eating them in the fall on her porch in the middle of Iowa. I've coped her recipe below, although there are two steps that she kept secret as you notice by the missing step numbers. I hope that you can discover waht the missing steps are!

Ingredients

  • 1 pastry for a 9 inch double crust
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 8 Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 Degreees F. While the oven is heating
    1. Melt the butter in a saucepan
    2. Stir in flour
    3. Add water, white and brown sugar
    4. Bring to boil
    5. Reduce temperatur and let simmer
  2. Place the bottom crust in the pan. Fill with apples. Cover with a lattice of crust
  3. Pour the sugar mixture over the crust
  4. Bake for 15 minutes
    1. Reduce the temperature to 350 degreees
    2. Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes until the apples are soft
